Our Story

Welcome to The Golden Espresso, your newest go-to spot for delicious coffee & treats! We serve a variety of hot and iced beverages, irresistable cakes & flavour-packed acai bowls to brighten your day. Whether you're craving the perfect morning brew or something sweet, we've got you covered! Come pay us a visit and treat yourself!



The Golden Espresso started with a simple idea: to share our passion for great tasting coffee and even better tasting treats. What began as a dream quickly became a reality, as we kickstarted our little coffee truck in 2022 in Trim, Co.Meath. From day one the mission has been to create a space where people can enjoy delicious coffee and food on the go. We wanted our customers to feel a warm sense of community every time they visited. Fast-forward to today, and we've done just that. We've grown not only our menu, but also a community of loyal customers. We've worked tirelessly to ensure that each creation that we serve is crafted with care, using only the highest quality of ingredients. Fueled by a passion for delivering quality, we're proud to be open 7 days a week to satisfy caffeine addicts & sweet tooths alike!



Our journey has been one of learning, improving and bringing joy to our customers' faces. So pop by - we would love to see you!
